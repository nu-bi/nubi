## Strength

Gold-standard enterprise semantic layer (LookML); deep Google Cloud / BigQuery integration; strong governance and access control; Gemini AI native (Conversational Analytics, LookML Assistant, Viz Assistant, Code Interpreter).

## Limitation

Extremely high LookML modeling tax — organisations spend est. 40–60% of total Looker investment on LookML dev/maintenance. No published pricing (opaque sales process). BigQuery compute costs stack on top of license. No self-host. No Arrow IPC. Embedding is a separate expensive SKU. AI Data Tokens billing begins Oct 2026 ($3/1M input, $20/1M output).

## Notes

Looker is the enterprise benchmark for semantic layers and governance. If you need LookML-grade governance at Google Cloud scale, Looker is still the reference. Nubi deliberately lowers that tax — no semantic model required to start — accepting some governance ceiling in exchange for time-to-dashboard.
