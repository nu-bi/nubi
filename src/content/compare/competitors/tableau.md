## Strength

Unmatched visualization breadth and polish; massive user community and training ecosystem; Hyper engine fast for columnar extracts; Salesforce CRM integration native; Tableau Agent (Cloud+) for agentic BI.

## Limitation

Every viewer is a paid seat — no viewer-only pricing for embeds below Enterprise OEM (entry ~$60k–$150k/year). Proprietary VizQL/Hyper format is not open. High cost at scale. No Arrow IPC. No automatic pre-aggregation (manual extract refresh scheduling). Cloud+ AI tier is opaque (contact sales).

## Notes

Tableau remains the benchmark for visualization polish. Salesforce's push toward Tableau Next (role-based, no consumption charges) is a step toward simpler pricing, but OEM embedding cost remains a barrier. Nubi targets the embedded-analytics buyer who finds Tableau OEM pricing prohibitive.
