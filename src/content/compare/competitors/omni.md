## Strength

Full-stack BI combining internal analytics authoring with embedded customer-facing analytics in one product; acquired Explo's embedded-analytics customer base and capabilities; strong SQL + no-code modeling; modern architecture.

## Limitation

No public pricing — requires a sales conversation; estimated prices are not verified. The per-viewer pricing model (~$420/viewer/year) is punitive for SaaS products with large user bases. Omni is relatively new and still integrating the Explo acquisition; product roadmap clarity is limited. Explo customers face migration risk. No self-host. No in-browser compute, no Arrow IPC.

## Notes

Omni represents the direction of convergence between internal BI and embedded analytics in a single platform. Pricing estimates are derived from AWS Marketplace listing and third-party sources — treat as indicative only. The Explo acquisition means customers who relied on Explo's standalone pricing (~$1,995/month) now face an unknown migration path. Nubi's transparent, self-serve pricing is a direct contrast to this model.
