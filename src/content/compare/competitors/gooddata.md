## Strength

Enterprise-grade platform with the longest track record in embedded analytics (founded 2008); strong governance, compliance (HIPAA, FedRAMP), and multi-tenant architecture; Analytics Lake for federated data; deep API for headless use; Kubernetes-native for on-prem enterprise deployments.

## Limitation

No public pricing — all tiers require a sales conversation; high implementation complexity; workspace-based pricing model can be difficult to predict for growing SaaS products. Primarily targeted at large enterprises; not accessible for SMB or early-stage SaaS products. No in-browser compute, no Arrow IPC. High minimum contract values (estimated $1,500/month floor, typically much higher for enterprise).

## Notes

GoodData is a strong competitor for large enterprise customers with compliance requirements (HIPAA, FedRAMP). For mid-market SaaS embedding, the opaque pricing and high entry cost make it inaccessible. The pricing figures here are estimates from third-party sources; GoodData does not publish prices publicly. Nubi's Pro tier ($149/month) undercuts the estimated GoodData Professional floor by over 10× with a comparable multi-tenant workspace and RLS model. Teams needing unlimited workspaces and compliance-grade governance can step up to Nubi Enterprise (from $1,000/month), which still comes in well below GoodData's typical entry cost.
