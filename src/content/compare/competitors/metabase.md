## Strength

Lowest barrier to entry for non-technical users; strong open-source community; free self-host (AGPL); new Data Studio semantic layer (v59, 2026); Metabot NL→SQL available on all tiers.

## Limitation

No viewer-only pricing tier — every embedded viewer is a full paid seat (1,000 embedded viewers ≈ $149 k/year at Pro rates). No WebGL or Arrow path. Pre-aggregation is manual only. Open Source requires AGPL compliance (may require open-sourcing custom modifications).

## Notes

Metabase is the closest open-source comparison to Nubi for non-technical users. The AGPL license is a compliance concern for SaaS products using Metabase as a component. Nubi's embedded viewer pricing is structurally different: near-zero marginal cost at high cache-hit rates vs Metabase's per-seat model.
