## Strength

Viewers are genuinely free at every tier — no viewer seat penalty. Per-editor-only pricing model is conceptually similar to Nubi's no-per-seat philosophy. Canvas-based (non-grid) layout is distinctive and suits narrative analytics. Strong SQL + Python + dbt integration.

## Limitation

Per-editor pricing at $49–$69/month still penalises team growth for larger authoring teams. Cloud-only with no self-host. Embedding is limited — not a core product surface; Enterprise is required for full embedding. No in-browser compute, no Arrow IPC, no WebGL path. Scale tier minimum 15-editor floor ($1,035/month) is a large jump from Pro.

## Notes

Count occupies a distinct niche: the collaborative "data canvas" (not grid dashboards). For teams doing narrative analytics with SQL and Python, Count is a compelling alternative. Count's free-viewer policy aligns with the direction Nubi takes to its extreme (truly unlimited users at every tier). For embedded SaaS, Count's embedding is limited and Enterprise-only.
